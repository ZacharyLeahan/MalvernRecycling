Green Resources

Resources

Native Plants
  • Choose Native Plants shows a full list of plants that are native to Pennsylvannia. We highly recommend the Quick Search tool if you are new to the site.
  • Northbrook Natives (West Chester, PA) is our local native plant design service and nursery.
  • Wild Ones Southeastern Pennsylvania (Southeastern PA) is the most local native plant group.
  • West Chester Plant Swap hosts plant swap events. Lots of native plants shared.
Townships' Efforts
  • Willistown Township has an "Environmental Sustainability Commission." You can read recent meeting minutes that they post on this site.
  • Malvern Bourgh Township has an "Environmental Advisory Council." You can read recent meeting minutes that they post on this site.
Green Teams Closeby
  • There is no Malvern, PA Green team. :-(
  • TE Green Team - Paoli area (Tredyffrin-Easttown). You can email them at TEGreenTeam@gmail.com
  • Phoenixville Green Team
Green Focused Stores
  • Community Eco Store in Phoenixville is a mecca of green focused products and information. The owners donate all profits back to the community.
Local Repair Cafes
  • Phoenixville Repair Cafe is our most local repair cafe. They host scheduled events where you can bring in up to 2 items to be repaired. They can fix electronics, clothes, dull blades, broken toys, etc.
Farmer's Markets
  • Our own Malvern Farmer's Market is at Burke Park in downtown Malvern.
  • West Chester Farmer's Market is 20 minutes west.
  • Phoenixville Farmer's Market is 20 minutes north.
Positive Change Organizations
  • Home of the Sparrow (Exton, PA) provides services to single women and mothers who are experiencing homelessness.
  • Chester County Food Bank helps our neighbors with food insecurity.
Vegetable Gardens
  • Chester County Food Bank's Gardens in the Community helps home gardeners grow food for the food bank.
  • Vegetable Gardens 4U (Malvern, PA) provides services to design and build residential vegetable gardens.
  • Jack's Farm (Pottstown, PA) sells vegetable plants that you can pick up at the Phoenixville Farmer's Market.
Recycling Websites
  • There's a Chester County SWA page for Chester County wide recycling resources.
